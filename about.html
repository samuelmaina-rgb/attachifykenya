About Attachify Kenya

Attachify Kenya is a platform dedicated to connecting university and college students with attachment opportunities across Kenya. We make it easier for students to discover opportunities while helping companies find talented future professionals.

Our Mission

To simplify the attachment search process by bringing opportunities from different industries into one convenient platform.

Our Vision

To become Kenya's leading platform for student attachment and internship opportunities.

Why Choose Us?

  • ✔ Easy to use
  • ✔ Student-friendly
  • ✔ Opportunities across Kenya
  • ✔ Connects students with employers
